Role-based access control

Budibase offers fully customizable RBAC. Give each user access to the exact screens and features they need, maximizing both usability and security. Assign different permission tiers based on location, job role, seniority, or any other user variable. With Budibase, it’s fast and easy to create fully tailored RBAC, to match your existing business processes. Budibase is built for security. Our platform offers free SSO, optional self-hosting, and customizable RBAC. We give our users total control over who accesses their apps, data, and workflows. We also offer full support for OAuth and OpenID.
