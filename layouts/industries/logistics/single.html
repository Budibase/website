Self-hosting and cloud deployments

Budibase gives you the power to choose the right hosting and deployment for your business. Choose self-hosting or our Budibase Cloud platform. Deploy to your own infrastructure using Kubernetes, Docker, Docker Compose, Digital Ocean, and more. With our flexible hosting options, enterprises around the world choose Budibase to build web tools, mobile apps, internal software, and more.

Automated business processes

Budibase is built to automate processes. Use any in-app action to trigger our library of built-in automations. Use handlebars expressions, third-party integrations, formula variables, and custom JavaScript to build your own powerful automation rules. Use our platform to automate logistics workflows, reduce operating costs, eliminate admin tasks, and optimize time-to-market.

External data support

Use Budibase to centralize existing datasets. Connect your Budibase tools to external data using MSSQL, MySQL, Postgresql, MongoDB, CouchDB, Airtable, Oracle, S3, RestAPI, and more. We also offer our own built-in database, with support for CSV uploads. Say goodbye to disorganized data. With Budibase, it’s easy to build intuitive apps for managing and leveraging a variety of datasets.

Role-based access control

Use custom RBAC to control how users interact with your Budibase tools. Assign different permission tiers, based on job role, seniority, location, department, or any other user variables. Use simple drop-down editors to limit individual app screens to any permission tier. Our custom RBAC offers maximum security and usability across all user groups.
