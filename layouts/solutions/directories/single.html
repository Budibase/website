What is a directory app?

Directory apps are used to store, manage, and share information and contact details for different individuals and companies. Businesses use directories to provide accessible, up-to-date records of their employees, suppliers, customers, and other stakeholders. Most directories are stand-alone tools, for users to look up, add, or update information. Others integrate with external tools, to provide a streamlined, efficient way to gather, manage, and access existing data. Use Budibase to build tools for fast, information access, saving time and money.

Why do you need a dedicated directory?

Businesses waste huge amounts of time and money sharing basic information among colleagues, customers, and partners. Quick calls, emails, and time spent searching through spreadsheets quickly add up to massive hidden costs. Directories put the information users need, right at their fingertips. Empower your team to quickly locate crucial data, without wasting coworkers’ time. Building a dedicated directory tool ensures that your data is consistent, accurate, and up-to-date. Give your team an intuitive interface to update and collect information, without the need for specialist database skills.
