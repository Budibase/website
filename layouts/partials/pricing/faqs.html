How does Budibase pricing work?

You can get started with Budibase today for free (cloud or self-host).

Once your team is ready to collaborate with more users, you can check out our Premium, Business and Enterprise plans based on your user need. User pricing ranges from $8-$16 per user (per annum), with custom pricing available for Enterprise plans.

What counts as a Budibase user?

A Budibase user is anyone who requires the ability to login to Budibase in order to build, edit or use Budibase applications. This means that all users can not only be end users for applications, but also actively collaborate with others during the application building process.

Does Budibase offer discounts?

Budibase is happy to offer eligible nonprofit organizations a discount across all our paid tiers on annual plans.

Volume based discounts are also available for Enterprise plans. Contact Sales to find out more.

Is Budibase free to use?

Budibase has multiple packages to help teams of all sizes orchestrate their work. Our Free plan is available to teams for no charge and provides everything you need to build internal tools and workflows in minutes. Budibase is also open-source, and you can run it on your own infrastructure for free. Our paid plans offer additional power and scale for teams as they grow and their needs get more complex.

You can find other Budibase builders in our community on our Discord.

Can I self-host Budibase for free?

Yes, you can self-host Budibase for free. Check out our self-host documentation for further guidance.

Enterprise customers can also benefit from on-premise deployments plus additional proprietary features.

Can I purchase one feature within the platform?

The Budibase platform is a collaborative solution for users to manage and manipulate their data, create applications, and build business workflows based on application triggers. We cannot separately provide functionality today.
