Team

Offboarding Tasks
HR team
  • Initiate offboarding when notice is received.
  • Confirm final working day and outstanding leave.
  • Update HRIS and benefits providers.
  • Prepare exit documentation and references.
  • Ensure compliance with retention policies.
Finance team
  • Process final payroll and benefits deductions.
  • Reconcile outstanding reimbursements or advances.
  • Deactivate any company cards or expense accounts.
  • Record any severance or settlement payments.
  • Confirm payroll data retention requirements.
Legal team
  • Review and confirm NDA and post-employment clauses.
  • Ensure intellectual property agreements are signed.
  • Advise on data access and retention requirements.
  • Support any dispute or compliance actions.
  • Sign off on the final exit documentation.
IT team
  • Revoke all system and VPN access.
  • Collect and wipe company hardware.
  • Transfer ownership of cloud accounts.
  • Archive or reassign email inboxes.
  • Remove from distribution lists and directories.
Line manager
  • Document and oversee handover of work.
  • Reassign responsibilities and client ownership.
  • Conduct exit interview or feedback session.
  • Retrieve access cards and physical assets.
  • Confirm completion of all offboarding tasks.
The leaver
  • Return all company property.
  • Clear personal files and data.
  • Submit handover notes or documentation.
  • Provide forwarding contact details.
  • Sign off on confidentiality and compliance forms.
