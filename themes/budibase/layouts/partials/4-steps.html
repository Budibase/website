Start to finish in 4 simple steps

Traditional development is time consuming - building web servers, writing boilerplate code, and wrestling with design takes weeks. Budibase removes the boilerplate work providing you with a platform to develop and design software 50x faster.

1. DATA

Add and manage your data with ease

With Budibase, you can connect to an external data source, import data, or start from scratch. You can then query, create views, and manage that data right within the Budibase GUI.

2. DESIGN

Build with pixel-perfect blocks

Budibase auto-generates beautiful screens, from your data, saving you precious dev time. It also comes with pixel-perfect, powerful components helping you build UIs users will love.

3. AUTOMATE

Automate manual processes

Save time by automating manual processes and workflows. From connecting to webhooks, to automating emails, simply tell Budibase what to do and let it work for you.

4. DEPLOY

Deploy your software with total control

In just a few simple clicks, you can deploy your software. Currently, you can only host with Budibase (AWS). We are currently working on self-hosting so please join the discussion and influence our direction.
