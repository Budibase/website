🎟️ Day passes explained

At Budibase, you only get billed for what you use, so you don’t pay for inactive users. With day passes, if a user uses Budibase within a 24 hr period, 1 day pass is used. Public users are free.

Team example:

John uses Budibase on Monday, Wednesday and Thursday

3 day passes

Lisa uses Budibase every weekday in September

22 day passes

Richard uses Budibase every Monday and Tuesday in September

8 day passes

Total day passes used by team

33 day passes
