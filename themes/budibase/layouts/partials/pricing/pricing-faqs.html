FAQs

Can't find the answer to your question, ask someone in the community on our Github Discussions Forum.

How does Budibase's pricing work?

The free and open source version of Budibase is available to use for free. Budibase Pro costs $4 per user per month when billed annually and $5 per user per month when billed monthly. The price of Budibase Business is $12 per user per month when billed annually and $15 when billed monthly.

Does Budibase offer a discount for nonprofits?

Budibase is happy to offer eligible nonprofit organizations a discount on a Budibase Pro or Budibase Business annual plan.

Can I self-host Budibase for free?

Yes, you can self-host Budibase for free. Check out our self-host docs for further guidance.

Do you charge for users?

On the free and open source, you are not charged for users. On the paid plans, we do charge per user.

Is Budibase free to use?

Budibase has multiple packages to help teams of all sizes orchestrate their work. Our Free plan is available to teams for no charge and provides everything you need to build internal apps and workflows in minutes. Budibase is also open-source, and you can run it on your own infrastructure for free. Our paid plans offer additional power and scale for teams as they grow and their needs get more complex.

What happens when I hit my usage limits?

If you reach our limits, you’ll still be able to use your apps. We’ll let you know about the overage, and give you a grace period to find a plan that fits your needs!

What are my payment options?

All payments are made via credit card using Stripe for Pro, Team, and Business workspaces. For Enterprise accounts, we can issue an annual invoice with payment via PO. Please contact sales for more information.

Can I deploy Budibase on-premise?

Yes! You can self-host Budibase on your own infrastructure. Enterprise customers can also benefit from on-premise deployments plus additional proprietary features.

Do you have a Service-Level Agreement (SLA)?

We do not offer a standard SLA. For enterprise orgs, we can offer a custom SLA. Contact sales to learn more.
