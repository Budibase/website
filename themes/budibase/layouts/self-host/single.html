    Do you charge for users?

    At Budibase, you only get billed for what you use, so you don’t pay for the members who aren’t using Budibase. Fair’s fair.

    What classifies a developer?

    Anyone who has the ability to create a new app with Budibase.

    We need to add new users to our team. How will that be billed?

    Any new user who logs in to an app or portal during a billing period will be billed for that period. So, if you a new member on the 20th of the month, and they use Budibase apps for the remainder of the month, we will bill you for that user for the entire month as they as a MAU.

    Can I buy additional queries, automation runs, storage?

    Yes! Please reach out and the sales team will be happy to assist.

    Can I deploy Budibase on-premise?

    Yes! You can self-host the community version of Budibase and the enterprise version.
