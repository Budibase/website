FAQs

Do you charge for end-users?

No, we do not charge based on the number of end-users you have.

What classifies a developer?

Anyone who has the ability to create a new app with Budibase.

We are glad you are growing and scaling with Budibase! Please contact us to discuss your resource requirements and we can customize a plan for your team.

Can I buy additional queries?

Yes! Please reach out and the sales team will be happy to assist.

Can I deploy Budibase on-premise?

Yes! You can self-host the community version of Budibase and the enterprise version.
