Custom legal workflow automation

With Budibase, it’s easy to build powerful, custom automations. Use any in-app action to trigger our vast automations library. Set automation rules with our built-in JavaScript editor, conditional UIs, third-party integrations, and formula variables. Add your SMTP credentials to trigger automated emails to clients, colleagues, and other stakeholders. Budibase is the smart way to build highly automated and efficient legal workflows.

External data support

Budibase offers extensive support for external datasets. Connect your legal management softwareto existing databases using MySQL, MSSQL, Postgresql, S3, Airtable, MongoDB, CouchDB, Oracle, RestAPI, and more. Query and manipulate a wide range of external data sources with our intuitive front-end interfaces. Use Budibase to build automations around your existing data. We also offer our own built-in database, with support for CSV uploads.

Role-based access control

Budibase offers fully customizable RBAC. Give each user the exact level of access they need. Use job roles, seniority, location, department, or any other user attribute to assign access to screens and data. Hide, display, or update any design element, based on individual users’ permission tier. Use custom RBAC from Budibase to create fully secure and intuitive legal workflow management tools.
