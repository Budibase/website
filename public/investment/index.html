Investment

Budibase raised a pre-seed round of £300,000 in December 2019 from Techstart NI. This pre-seed round allowed us the opportunity to explore the low / no code market and build a product which provided compound value.

Our next raise - seed round

To date, Budibase has over 15,000 signups from Philips, PWC, Deloitte, KPMG, Swiss, F1, the list goes on. We have surveyed over 4,000 of those signups and interviewed over 80. We understand them; their situation, their pains, what value means to them and we've created a product around this research.

We will soft-launch in mid-October and publicly launch in late November. Our product is ready for the market and we have the facilities in place to measure its value.

We seek to raise $2,000,000. With this investment, we will hit product market fit.

If you'd like to talk to us about investment in Budibase, please drop us a line at investors@budibase.com
